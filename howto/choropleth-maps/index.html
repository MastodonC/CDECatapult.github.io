Identifying diabetes prevalence and GP prescription trends

The Challenge

Over 2.4 million people in England or about 5.5% have diabetes. On average £24.45 is spent per diabetes patient per month on prescriptions to treat their condition.

The Solution

This site takes data from sources like the Health and Social Care Information Centre (HSCIC) and shows how it can be turned into visualisations and analysis to answer specific questions for your organisation.

This is the ALPHA version of the site, which shows how to take a small number of datasets including data about GP prescriptions and diabetes prevalence to show the extent of diabetes treatment in the UK.
