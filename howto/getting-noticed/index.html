Getting your project noticed

So you have a great idea for a digital project, but how do you go about making sure your creation is noticed and talked about by the right people? The Digital Catapult communications team have created a short guide with a few suggestions on how to get your app, program or website in front of the people who need to see it. Use the guide to navigate your way through the challenges of generating wider awareness and adoption of your product, app, website or programme. The guide covers: reaching out to your network; social media; website; design; dealing with trolls; keeping up with industry trends; reaching out to journalists; experimenting with video and hackathons, and more!
